About

I am a full stack web developer experienced with numerous programming languages and technologies. Have worked on a wide variety of applications that have given me a good understanding of both front-end and back-end technologies. I enjoy challenges that allow me to build and design real world applications that equip users with the tools they need to succeed. I hold a bachelor’s degree in Criminal Justice from The University of North Carolina at Charlotte. My passion for learning and always moving forward in life, lead me to return to school and I have successfully completed and earned a Full Stack Web Development Certificate from UNC Charlotte.

During my time in the course, I have gained many valuable skills. I am proficient in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, JQuery, Node.js, GIT, GitHub, various style frameworks including Bootstrap and Materialize. I additionally have experience with API’s, Express, React, Handlebars, Node and have worked with databases such as Firebase, MySQL with Sequelize and MongoDB with Mongoose.
